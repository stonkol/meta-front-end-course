Our Story

The Little Lemon Experience

A culinary journey through the Mediterranean

Little Lemon brings the essence of Mediterranean cuisine to your table with a contemporary approach.

Founded in 2010 by award-winning chef Maria Rossi, Little Lemon has become a culinary landmark known for its commitment to authentic flavors and innovative presentations.

We source only the freshest ingredients from local farmers and Mediterranean importers to ensure each dish captures the true essence of the region.
